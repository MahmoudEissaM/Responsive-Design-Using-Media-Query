ITI Zagazig

Over 30 years, ITI has kept updating its training portfolio through continuous monitoring for the future global tech trends, as well as the country’s national and mega projects, hence; sustaining its graduates’ employment percentage to 85 prior their graduation.

Activities

mission
Supporting Egyptian youth to improve their career opportunities by developing their skills and preparing them for highly in-demand jobs, and related vast technologies to fulfill today’s needs, and tomorrow’s forecast.
